Research

Hello everyone! My name is Aaron Collavini, I am currently pursuing a double PhD degree in physics (University of Udine) and philosophy (University of Geneve). I am affiliated with the National Institute of Nuclear Physics, and member of the Geneva Symmetry Group.

My research focuses on the foundations of gravitational theories, with particular attention to the geometrical structure of space-time and its interplay with matter.
