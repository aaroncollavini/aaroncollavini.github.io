About me

Hello everyone!

I am an Italian PhD student pursuing a joint PhD in Physics at the University of Udine and in Philosophy at the University of Geneva, and I have also been a visiting researcher at the University of California, Irvine. I am affiliated with the National Institute of Nuclear Physics, member of the Geneva Symmetry Group and co-founder of the Philosophy and Foundations of Physics Seminar at the University of Udine.

I obtained an M.Sc. in Physics from the University of Trieste in 2022 and an M.A. in Philosophy from the University of Italian Switzerland in 2025.

My research focuses on the foundations of gravitational theories, with particular attention to the geometrical structure of space-time and its interplay with matter. In particular, I am currently investigating the conceptual and mathematical foundations of torsion theories of gravity.
